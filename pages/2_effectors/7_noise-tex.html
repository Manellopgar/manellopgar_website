Effector Noise Texture

This effector utilizes a Noise Texture to modify the Clones.

Effector Noise Texture - Min/Max

Noise Settings

  • Disable: Disables the Noise options.
  • Enable: Enables the Noise options.

Noise Type

  • Multifractal: Animation driven by the Multifractal Noise Texture.
  • Ridged Multifractal: Animation driven by the Ridged Multifractal Noise Texture.
  • Hybrid Multifractal: Animation driven by the Hybrid Multifractal Noise Texture.
  • fBM: Animation driven by the fBM Noise Texture.
  • Hetero Terrain: Animation driven by the Hetero Terrain Noise Texture.

Auto Animation

  • Disable: Disables the Animation options.
  • Enable: Enables the Animation options.

Animation Speed: Adjusts the Animation Speed.

Loop Animation

  • Disable: Disables the Loop Animation options.
  • Enable: Enables the Loop Animation options.

Frame Loop Out: Enter the End Frame for the Loop Out. If negative, it does not loop.

Noise Seed: Changes the Seed of the Noise.

Invert Noise Texture: Inverts the values of the Noise Texture.

Detail: Amount of distortion noise detail.

Roughness: Blends between a smoother noise pattern and one with rougher, sharper peaks.

Distortion: Amount of distortion.

Uniform Scale: Uniform Scale of the Noise Texture.

Noise Scale: Noise Scale XYZ values.
