Effector Time

As the name suggests, this Effector uses the Scene Time to modify the clones.

Effector Time - Example

Time Settings

Show the Time Settings options.

  • Switch Frame / Seconds: Switch the Auto Animation between Timeline Frames or Seconds.
  • Multiply/Division: Multiply or Divide the Animation Speed.
  • Negative: Use Negative Animation.
  • Animation Speed: Adjust the Animation Speed.
  • Rel Offset: Enable the Relative Offset for the Animation.
  • Offset Time: Offset the Time Animation.
  • Randomize Time: Randomize the Time Animation Between Clones.
