Effector Push Apart

Mode

Choose the Push Apart Mode

  • Hide Mode: Clones will not be moved. They will be hidden. It may happen that more clones are hidden than is actually necessary.
  • Push Apart Mode: When in this mode, an attempt will be made - depending on the Radius value defined - to create the defined minimum distance by pushing the clones apart. If the clones lie too closely together, the entire clone group will be inflated spatially (instead of the clones "looking for" free spaces).
  • Scale Apart Mode: As soon as the virtual spheres (see Radius) intersect, the respective clones will be scaled down. The greater the intersection, the more the clones will be scaled down. Clones that lie close together will be made smaller the larger the Radius value becomes.
  • Along X Axis Mode: In principle, this works similarly to Push Apart only that the clones are pushed apart only along their X axis.
  • Along Y Axis Mode: In principle, this works similarly to Push Apart only that the clones are pushed apart only along their Y axis.
  • Along Z Axis Mode: In principle, this works similarly to Push Apart only that the clones are pushed apart only along their Z axis.

Radius

Imagine a sphere with this radius around each clone. If these radii intersect, the Push Apart Effector will react accordingly, depending on which Mode is selected.
