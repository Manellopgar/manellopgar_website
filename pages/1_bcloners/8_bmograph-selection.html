BMograph Selection

This tool allows you to select specific Clones from any BCloner, BFracture, BMoText, or any tool that generates instances. You can use indices, custom meshes, empties, or even indices for selection. Additionally, you can store the inverted selection as well. All selections are stored in the instances for quick use with Effectors or any tool that has the Custom Selection parameter.

Display

Displays the Asset Indices in the Viewport, making it easier to identify which index each instance has.

  • None: Does not display Clone Indices.
  • Index: Displays the Clone Indices.

BMograph Selection - Display

Type of Selection

Choose the type of selection method.

  • Range of Indices: Allows the selection of a range of Clones by their Indices.
  • Object/Collection: Allows the selection of a volume of Objects/Collections by their Indices.
  • Fields: Allows the use of a Field as a selection of Clones by their Indices.
  • 10 Custom Indices: Allows the selection of up to 10 Custom Indices of Clones.
  • 10 Range of Indices: Allows the selection of up to 10 Ranges of Indices of Clones.
  • Even Index: Allows the selection of the Even Indices of Clones.

Range of Selection

Less Than: Selects the Indices that are less than this number.

Greater Than: Selects the Indices that are greater than this number.

BMograph Selection - Range of Selection

Object and Collection Selection

Object Selection: Add a Mesh for Custom Selection.

Collection Selection: Add a Collection of Mesh Objects or Empties for Custom Selection.

10 Range of Indices

Number of Custom Index: Choose the number of selections between 2 and 10 different index selections.

Custom Index 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06, 07, 08, 09, 10: Defines the number of the specific index (01-10).

Greater Than 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 06, 07, 08, 09, 10: Selects the indices that are greater than this number (01-10).
