BMoGraph Selection

This tool enables precise selection of specific instances generated by any BCloner, BFracture, BMoText, or similar instance-generating tools. It offers versatile selection methods including Indices, Custom Meshes, Empties, and Even Index filtering. You can also store inverted selections for quick reuse with Effectors or other tools that support the Custom Selection parameter.

Display Options

Note: This setting controls the visualization of Asset Indices in the Viewport, making it easier to identify which index each instance holds.

  • •
    None: Doesn't show the Clone Indices.
  • •
    Index: Shows the Clones Indices.

Type of Selection

Choose the method to define your selection criteria.

  • •
    Range of Indices: Allows the selection of a Range of Clones by its Indices.
  • •
    Object/Collection: Allows the selection of a Volume of Object/Collection of Objects by its Indices.
  • •
    Fields: Allows the use of a Field as selection of Clones by its Indices.
  • •
    10 Custom Indices: Allows the selection up to 10 Custom Indices of Clones.
  • •
    10 Range of Indices: Allows the selection up to 10 Range of Indices of Clones.
  • •
    Even Index: Allows the selection of the Even Indices of Clones.

Range Selection Parameters

Use these parameters to define indices based on numerical comparison.

  • •
    Less Than: Selects the Indices that are Less Than this Number.
  • •
    Greater Than: Selects the Indices that are Greater Than this Number.

Object & Collection Selection

Select instances based on external geometry references.

  • •
    Object Selection: Add a Mesh for Custom Selection.
  • •
    Collection Selection: Add a Collection of Mesh Objects or Empties for Custom Selection.

Object and Collection Selection Example

Custom Indices & Ranges

Advanced selection for specific or multiple index groups.

  • •
    Nº of Custom Index: Choose the Number of Selections between 2 up to 10 different index selections.
  • •
    Custom Index 01-10: Defines the specific Number of the Index (e.g., 01, 02, ... 10).
  • •
    Nº of Range of Selection: Choose the Number of Range of Selections.
  • •
    Less Than (01-10): Selects the Indices that are Less Than this Number for each range.
  • •
    Greater Than (01-10): Selects the Indices that are Greater Than this Number for each range.

Custom and Range Selection Example

Field & Advanced Options

  • •
    Name of Selection: Write your Custom Name Field if you want to use it with a Specific Name.
  • •
    Field Tolerance: Adjusts the Map Range of the Field Values.

Storage & Conversion

Manage your selections for future use or conversion.

  • •
    Store Invert Selection: Stores the Inverted Selection to use with any B4D Tool.
  • •
    Convert Selection To Instance: Converts the actual Selection to A Instance.
