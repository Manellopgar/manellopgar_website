Initial Guide for All Fields

In this guide, I explain how to add any Effector and the common parameters that work consistently across all Effectors. For details on specific parameters, please refer to the dedicated menus for each specific Effector.

Note: By default, all modifiers operate in a quick and simple version. To access advanced options for any modifier, you must enable the Advanced Settings/Parameters option in the addon preferences or panel.

? How to add any Field?
▼

Select any mesh object, go to the BCloners Addon panel, and click any BCloner button. The selected BCloner and its associated Transform modifier are added automatically.

The Fields Modifiers Order

In the Modifier Tab, the correct order is almost always from top to bottom. The Addon typically sets modifiers in the optimal sequence automatically. However, there are cases where you may need to rearrange them manually.

Visual examples demonstrating the correct modifier stacking order.

How to use the Outliner Collections created by the addon?

The BCloners Addon automatically generates organized collections in the Outliner to help you manage your clones and effector objects efficiently. These collections are structured to streamline your workflow, allowing for quick selection, hiding, or isolating specific groups of objects without cluttering your viewport.
